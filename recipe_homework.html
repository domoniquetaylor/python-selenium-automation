How to Make Blueberry Cheesecake Pancakes

Ingredients

  • pancake batter
  • three eggs
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 4 oz of cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of blueberries
  • 1/4 cup Land O Lakes Butter (with Canola Oil)
  • 2 tsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest

Instructions

    1. Place egg whites into bowl. Beat at high speed until stiff peaks form; set aside.
    2. Beat at high speed until stiff peaks form; set aside.
    3. Combine egg yolks, butter, sugar, vanilla and salt in bowl; beat until well mixed.
    4. Add cream cheese; beat until just combined, leaving chunks of cream cheese intact.
    5. Add milk; stir until just combined.
    6. Add flour; mix until just combined.
    7. Gently fold in egg whites and blueberries.
    8. Heat griddle or large skillet over medium heat.
    9. Combine Butter with Canola Oil, powdered sugar and lemon zest in bowl; mix well. Set aside.
    10. Spoon 1/3 cupfuls of batter onto griddle; cook 5 minutes or until bubbles form on surface.
    11. Turn; continue cooking 3 minutes or until deep golden brown. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
    12. Serve immediately with lemon butter.
